MENTOR A TEAM

Our participants rely on the guidance and advice of experienced mentors. These mentors augment our participants' tenacity and knowledge with experience and connections. Our mentors are professional engineers, physicians, and entrepreneurs who are domain experts or generalists. Regardless, all of our mentors ultimately enable our participants' success. We would love to get you involved by:

  • giving quarterly feedback to teams as a panel mentor for our "Design Reviews"
  • giving feedback when necessary by engaging directly with teams

To get invoved, please email us!
