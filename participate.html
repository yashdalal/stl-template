APPLY TO JOIN A TEAM

If you'd like to join or lead a team, please apply here. You will have the option to apply as either a team leader or a participant. Our program runs from September to May, but you are welcome to apply out of cycle so that we can contact you later! In special situations, especially motivated teams have been allowed to enter the program mid-cycle.
